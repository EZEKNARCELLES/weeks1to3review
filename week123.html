What is the Strategy Execution Gap?

It's the persistent and costly disconnect between what an organization aspires to achieve (its strategy) and what it actually accomplishes (its execution).

This isn't a small problem. Most organizations fail to achieve a significant portion of their strategic goals, leading to wasted resources, lost competitive ground, and disengaged employees.
